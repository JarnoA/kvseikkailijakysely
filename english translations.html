Characters


You are a Dreamer Dragonfly!

Your eyes have been opened to the wrongs of the world and you want to put right what's wrong. You are aware of your small size but don't let it get in the way of dreaming of change. You gather together with others to make your voice stronger. You're weighed down by inequality in the world, but you can also imagine a better future.

Check out at least the following: The European Solidarity Corps’ solidarity projects (in Finnish) and volunteer projects (in Finnish) as well as international training.

Illustration by: Minttu Nurmi



You are a Developer Moth!

You know where you're headed, even if you haven't seen the whole route yet. You have a way of also getting others to try things that are not yet possible. As you flutter towards new networks, you dream about concepts and methods, seminars, influencing decision-makers and bringing different perspectives together. You turn project ideas into solutions to the problems that you face.

Check out at least the following: Erasmus+ partnerships for cooperation (in Finnish) and international training.

Illustration by: Minttu Nurmi


You are a Coordinating Caterpillar

You measure the world from where you are and can easily spot where your help is needed. You spring elegantly from manual to reporting form. You’re an important part of your population’s activities, as without you the wheels wouldn't turn or the ideas wouldn't reach the finish line.

Check out at least the following: Briefings for Erasmus+ projects (in Finnish) and European Solidarity Corps projects (in Finnish), international training.

Illustration by: Minttu Nurmi


You are an Adventure Cricket!

You've been involved in many things and have international experience, both abroad and at home. But your thirst for adventure hasn’t been sated yet, which is why you're already exploring the next opportunities to hop on a train and discover new landscapes to explore alone or with others! The moments when you see young people's own wings carrying them make up for all the emergency landings on the rocks.

Check out at least the following: Erasmus+ mobility of youth workers (in Finnish), DiscoverEU (in Finnish), international training.

Illustration by: Minttu Nurmi


You are an Encourager Beetle!

You make sure that everyone feels heard and seen. You are ready to help others in every situation. You shower people with words of encouragement and know the paths and opportunities ahead. You give those around you, both young people and your colleagues, the courage to spread their wings in the face of new things!

Check out at least the following: European Solidarity Corps’ solidarity projects (in Finnish), Erasmus+ youth participation activities (in Finnish), Eurodesk youth information network.

Illustration by: Minttu Nurmi



You are a Participation Bee!

You're inspired by young people finding new ways to make their voices heard. You've made it your business to make sure that every little creature in the garden has its place in the group. You know what kind of supported routes to the world are available, or at least you're determined to find out about them.

Check out at least the following: European Solidarity Corps’ volunteer projects (in Finnish), Erasmus+ youth exchanges (in Finnish).

Illustration by: Minttu Nurmi
